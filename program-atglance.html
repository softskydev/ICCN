10 - 14 September 2024 / Jakarta

ICCN 2024 : PROGRAMME AT GLANCE

Welcome to ICCN 2024, an Event designed to enrich the experience and knowledge in Clinical Neurophysiology. This event is designed to increase skills and knowledge as well as provide new experiences in the field of clinical neurophysiology. With various topics will be presented, the workshop will provide various themes with a wide range of practical skills in clinical neurophysiology and symposium will provide a comprehensive view of the latest developments in various related fields.
