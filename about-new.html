About ICCN 2024

10 - 14 September 2024 / Jakarta

INTERNATIONAL FEDERATION OF CLINICAL NEUROPHYSIOLOGY
ICCN 2024, JAKARTA

The International Federation of Clinical Neurophysiology (IFCN) and the Indonesian Neurological Association (PERDOSSI) are pleased to announce the 33rd International Congress of Clinical Neurophysiology will be held September 10-14, 2024 in Jakarta, Indonesia.

The ICCN 2024 program will include several signature courses, as well as symposia and workshops planned by IFCN Member Societies and other prominent societies in clinical neurophysiology from around the world. Congress courses and workshops are designed to provide a solid review of the fundamentals and the latest scientific advances in both central and peripheral clinical neurophysiology.

Through this activity, we hope that all participants could continuously grow and update their knowledge in the field of neurology, especially in clinical neurophysiology.
